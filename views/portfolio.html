89 Robotics

89 Robotics is a startup out of Northwestern University which aims to augment the security guard role with high-tech drones. I developed the proprietary cloud backend for low-latency streaming of controls and data.

ERD Studio

Due to my interest in computer science education, I worked on an extracurricular project to create a web app to teach databases and relational algebra. I created a drag-and-drop interface to generate entity-relationship diagrams.

Charity Chain

A long-term pet project, I developed a barebones full-stack web app to crowdsource "microdonations" with a social network-style interface to encourage user engagement and maximize charitable donations.

AI Reddit Classifier

My final project for EECS 349 Machine Learning was to program the best possible classifier to predict the popularity of Reddit posts. We added an additional challenge by choosing not to use unigrams or bigrams of the text, and instead focus on other features we could extract from the user and other properties of the post.

Artificial Intelligence Projects

While we're talking about AI, EECS 348 Artificial Intelligence involved numerous hands-on projects including a text sentiment classifier trained on movie reviews, sudoku and tic-tac-toe solvers, and weather prediction algorithms.

Geovibes

Part of a student software development team for EECS 394, we created a mobile app that compared the mood of various parts of the country by performing sentiment analysis on tweets from that area.
